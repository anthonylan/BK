Creative Producer + Director of Photography

As long as I can remember, I have been pursuing not just a career in video production and filmmaking but a life dedicated to telling stories through compelling images.

After graduating from Columbia College Chicago with a BA in filmmaking and entertainment business, I have been fortunate to travel the country working in myriad roles in Hollywood features, prestige television shows, branded content for Fortune 500 companies, and live streaming premiere music festivals.

In 2017, I combined two of my major passions of animals and video, when I joined Chewy.com as a Creative Producer. Since then, I have helped to build a video studio, managed multiple creative pivots and oversaw the production of nearly 10,000 videos.

When I’m not hiking, rock climbing, or at the movie theater, I can be found spending quality time with my two crazy dogs and my beautiful partner. My travels have brought me to the Carribean Islands, Cuba, and Australia where I’ve documented the incredible beauty of our planet.
